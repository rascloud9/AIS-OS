# Reference: Plante Moran Valuation Engagement Letter (Hallmark Management Service)

**Type:** Reference / comparable engagement
**Source PDF:** `VALUATION engagement - Hallmark Management - Plante Moran 9-16-21.pdf` (drop into this folder — see note at bottom)
**Date:** September 15, 2021
**Why it's here:** A real, signed business-valuation engagement letter. Useful as a template/benchmark for what a TSD valuation engagement would scope, cost, and require.

## Parties
- **Firm:** Plante & Moran, PLLC ("PM")
- **Valuator:** Jesse L. Richardson, ASA, CFE (Partner)
- **Client:** Hallmark Management Service, Inc. — Tom Walters & Bryan West (2323 W. 5th Ave, Suite 140, Columbus, OH 43204)

## Scope of services
- **Entity valued:** Hallmark Management Service, Inc.
- **Nature of interest:** Fair Market Value of a **50% interest**, on a **non-marketable, non-controlling** basis
- **Purpose:** Management planning / stock transaction
- **Valuation date:** TBD / current
- **Standard of value:** Fair market value per IRS Rev. Ruling 59-60
- **Standards conformity:** USPAP, ASA Principles of Appraisal Practice, AICPA SSVS
- **Deliverable:** Detailed written report; schedules within 4–6 weeks of receiving all needed info

## Fees & terms
- **Fee range:** $12,000–$15,000 plus costs
- **First invoice:** $5,000 on signed engagement letter
- Approval required if scope exceeds $15,000; meetings/IRS/testimony billed hourly
- Late charge 1.25%/month after 30 days
- Governing law: Ohio; claims within 2 years; remote-first engagement

## Relevance to TSD buyout
- **Benchmark cost** for a third-party valuation: ~$12–15K.
- **Interest type matters** — Hallmark valued a 50% non-controlling, non-marketable stake (subject to discounts). Mirror the framing to TSD's deal structure (see [../financials.md](../financials.md)).
- Plante Moran / Jesse Richardson is a known local (Columbus/Southfield) valuation source if Rick needs an independent appraisal.

## The actual deal that followed

This engagement produced the valuation, and the buyout closed in March 2022. Both the **valuation report** and the **signed stock redemption agreement** are now on disk and synthesized in **[hms-comparable-deal-2021-2022.md](hms-comparable-deal-2021-2022.md)** — appraised $1,431,800, closed $1,233,300 (35% cash / 65% seller note).
