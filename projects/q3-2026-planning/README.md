## What this is

EOS-style quarterly planning for the **TSD business unit**. Sets the back-half-of-year goals and the 90-day Rocks for Q3. Draft Rocks are seeded from this quarter's stated priorities — refine, cut, and assign owners.

**Scope discipline:** the focus is on what TSD can own and move. Overarching Revolution Group company-level items (e.g., SOC audit) are kept in clearly separated sections so they're tracked as dependencies without crowding out TSD's own priorities.

## EOS quick reference

- **Rocks** = the 3-7 most important things to finish in 90 days. Fewer is better. Each has one owner.
- **Annual goals** = the 1-year targets the Rocks ladder up to.
- **Scorecard** = 5-15 weekly numbers; leading indicators, not lagging.
- **Issues** = anything in the way; solved in the weekly meeting, not the planning session.
