# About the Business

## What we do and who we serve

Revolution Group provides Managed IT Security and Support to SMB and mid-market companies in Central Ohio. SMB clients get fully-managed IT — we act as their IT department. Mid-market clients get co-managed services: their internal IT handles Level 1 helpdesk while we manage the network, hosted security, hosted servers, and services. Currently ~50 clients on monthly MSP subscriptions. The TSD division is ~30 people, fully remote.

## Revenue model

Recurring monthly MSP fees billed through HaloPSA, integrated with QuickBooks. Payments received and financials tracked in QuickBooks (P&L, A/R, A/P). A monthly rolling financial spreadsheet is used for analysis alongside QuickBooks reporting.
